About Me

Welcome to The Polis Mindset! I'm Nonit Tilokani, currently pursuing my Bachelor's in Political Science at the prestigious University of Delhi. My academic journey has fostered a deep passion for political theory, international relations, and comparative politics.

As a student of political science, I've developed a keen interest in analyzing contemporary political phenomena through various theoretical frameworks. This platform serves as a medium to share academic insights and foster intellectual discussions in the field of humanities.

Through The Polis Mindset, I aim to create a comprehensive resource hub for fellow students and scholars interested in political science, history, economics, and social theory. My goal is to make complex academic concepts more accessible while maintaining their scholarly rigor.
